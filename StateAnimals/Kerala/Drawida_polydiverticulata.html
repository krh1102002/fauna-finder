Drawida polydiverticulata

Kingdom Animalia
Phylum Annelida
Class Clitellata
Sub-Class Oligochaeta
Order Haplotaxida
Family Moniligastridae
Genus Drawida
Species D. polydiverticulata

Drawida polydiverticulata is a species of earthworm from family Moniligastridae found from shola grasslands of the Munnar region in Kerala.

D. polydiverticulata has multiple lobes, also called diverticulums; an organ located in the front of its body, are unique amongst the members of the genus.
