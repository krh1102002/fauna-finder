Himalayan Vulture

Kingdom Animalia
Phylum Chordata
Class Mammalia
Order Artiodactyla
Family Plataistidae
Genus Platanista
Species P. gangetica

The Himalayan vulture (Gyps himalayensis) or Himalayan griffon vulture is an Old World vulture native to the Himalayas and the adjoining Tibetan Plateau. It is one of the two largest Old World vultures and true raptors. It is listed as Near Threatened on the IUCN Red List.[1] It is not to be confused with the griffon vulture, which is a similar species.
