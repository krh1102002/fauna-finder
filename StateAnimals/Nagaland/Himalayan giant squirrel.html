Himalayan giant squirrel

Kingdom Animalia
Phylum Chordata
Class Mammalia
Order Rodentia
Family Sciuridae
Genus Ratufa
Species R. indica

Himalayan giant squirrel is a large multi-coloured tree squirrel species endemic to forests and woodlands in India. It is found at altitudes of 180–2,300 m (590–7,550 ft) in tropical deciduous and moist evergreen forests and woodlands.
