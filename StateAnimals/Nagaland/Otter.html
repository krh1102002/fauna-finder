Otter

Kingdom Animalia
Phylum Chordata
Class Mammalia
Order Carnivora
Family Mustelidae
Sub-Family Lutrinae

Otters are carnivorous mammals in the subfamily Lutrinae. For most otters, fish is the staple of their diet. This is often supplemented by frogs, crayfish and crabs. Otters have long, slim bodies and relatively short limbs. Several otter species live in cold waters
